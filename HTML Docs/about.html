About Us

Africa Ability Trust is a registered nonprofit organization in Kenya, empowering Persons with Disabilities and caregivers by equipping communities, organizations and churches to build inclusive spaces of dignity and hope.

Africa Ability Trust is based in Kibera, just five kilometers southwest of Nairobi. Though it spans only 2.5 square kilometers, Kibera is home to nearly one million people, making it one of the most densely populated places in the world.

In the heart of this vibrant yet underserved community, we walk with families navigating disability, poverty, and social stigma. Our work is rooted in proximity, dignity, and lasting transformation.

Our Approach

We go beyond charity. We journey with families, strengthen faith communities, and reframe disability through the lens of faith and belonging.

We focus on visibility, we pursue authentic inclusion, transformative discipleship, and lasting dignity.
