Ways You Can Get Involved

Volunteer Your Time or Skills

Offer your unique gifts to support our work, whether in caregiving, teaching, administration, graphic or web design, event planning, media, legal advice, baking, sewing, or mentorship. Every contribution brings light and strength to our mission.

Engage in Community Outreach

Join us in outreach efforts such community awareness, evangelism, home visits, advocacy campaigns, inclusive events, and recreational activities. Help us build bridges of compassion and connection.

Lift Us in Prayer

Pray for those we serve to experience God’s love and salvation, for strength and wisdom in our work, and for churches and the community to become places where every person is welcomed and valued.

Give Financial or Material Support

Your generosity enables us to serve more families, to build inclusive communities where every person, regardless of ability, is valued & empowered. Financial donations and material gifts, like food, clothing, mobility aids, or household supplies, go a long way in uplifting lives with dignity.
